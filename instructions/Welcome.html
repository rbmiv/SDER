No Talking Allowed

Now that the experiment has begun, we ask that you do not communicate with other participants. If you have any questions after we finish reading the instructions, please raise your hand and the experimenter will answer your question in private.

Privacy

This experiment is structured so that no one, including the experimenters or the other participants, will ever know your personal decision or money earnings in the experiment. This is accomplished by giving you the only key to a numbered mailbox that contains a sealed envelope with your earnings inside.

This experiment is structured so that no one, including the experimenters, the monitor, and the other subjects will ever know the personal decision of anyone in the experiment. Every person's privacy is guaranteed because neither his/her name nor student ID number will appear on any form that records decisions in this experiment.

One Round

There will be one decision task in this experiment. You will face the decision task only once, so you should take it seriously.

Random Matching and Anonymity

You will be randomly matched with 3 other people to form a group of 4 individuals that will stay the same for the entire decision task.

Cash Payoffs

Your earnings in this experiment are expressed in Experimental Currency Units, which we will refer to as ECUs. At the conclusion of the experiment you will be paid in U.S. dollars using a conversion rate of $1 for every {{exchange_rate}} ECU{{if exchange_rate != 1}} 's {{endif}} of earnings from the experiment.
